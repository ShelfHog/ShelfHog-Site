Privacy Policy

ShelfHog (“we”, “our”) · Last updated: April 30, 2026

This policy describes how ShelfHog collects, uses, and shares information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”). The Services help eBay sellers view sales and order information from linked eBay accounts in one place. We are not eBay Inc. eBay has its own privacy policy.

ShelfHog today: The app is currently offered free of charge. ShelfHog’s connection to eBay is read-only for viewing data we sync. You cannot place orders, accept purchases, or change listings through ShelfHog—those actions happen only on eBay (or other tools you use), not in our app, at least for now.

Contact

Questions: support@shelfhog.com
Support page: Support

Information we collect

  • eBay account connection. When you choose to connect an eBay store, eBay shares OAuth tokens (access and refresh) and identifier(s) for your eBay account. We store these so we can retrieve order data on your behalf until you disconnect or tokens expire.
  • Order and listing-related data (read-only). We retrieve and store information needed to show dashboards, store statistics, and “pull” or ship-queue style lists. This may include order IDs, dates, amounts, fulfillment status, line item titles, SKUs, and similar fields returned by eBay APIs. We use this data only to provide the Services to you.
  • Technical data. Like most hosted software, our infrastructure may process server logs, IP addresses, device/browser type, and diagnostic data for security and reliability (via our hosting providers, for example Fly.io).

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate and sync data from eBay accounts you connect.
  • Respond to support requests and protect against fraud or abuse.
  • Comply with law when required.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • eBay — when you use OAuth, data flows under eBay’s and your authorization.
  • Service providers — such as cloud hosting (e.g. servers and managed databases) that process data on our instructions.
  • Legal — if we believe disclosure is required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Retention & deletion

We keep OAuth tokens and synced order data while your store stays connected or as needed to run the Services. When you disconnect a store through ShelfHog, we remove that store’s tokens and associated synced data from our systems, consistent with our technical architecture (e.g. cascade delete per store).

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and access controls on production systems. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Disconnect eBay accounts in the app or via support instructions we provide.
  • Contact support@shelfhog.com for access, correction, or deletion requests where applicable law applies.

U.S. state privacy (summary)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to know, access, delete, or opt out of certain processing of personal information. Contact us at the email above. We do not “sell” or “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as commonly defined in CPRA.

International

If you access the Services from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S. or other countries where we or our providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new date at the top when we do. Continued use of the Services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
