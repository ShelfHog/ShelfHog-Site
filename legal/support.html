Support

ShelfHog · Help & contact

ShelfHog is currently a free app. eBay integration is read-only for viewing synced data—you cannot place orders or change listings here; do that on eBay.

Email: support@shelfhog.com

We aim to respond to account, technical, and privacy requests as soon as reasonably possible.

Common topics

  • Connect eBay: Use “Connect eBay” in the app. You must allow ShelfHog read access when eBay prompts you.
  • Disconnect a store: Use the disconnect option in the app for that store, or email us with your account details.
  • Data & privacy: See our Privacy Policy for what we collect and how to request help.
  • Terms: See our Terms of Use.

Bug reports

When reporting a problem, include your platform (iOS/Android), app version if shown, what you tried, and any error message (screenshot optional). Send to support@shelfhog.com.
